Hello and welcome. My name is Henry Mahnke, the photographer behind Sol Studio. Sol Studio is an exhibit of human connection to beauty wherever it is found. This is a story about our interrelation with the physical spaces around us, how they shape our identities, foster vitality, and constitute a canvas for existence. As a climber, surfer, skier, and hiker natural environments are a studio for self-determination and personal expression. These spaces are a medium for fulfillment and connection to the world around us. For years I have revered the Sun as a symbol of this sentiment, and a reminder to be tenacious and intrepid. It is no coincidence that as the brightest light in the universe, Sol sounds like soul.

This portfolio showcases some of my recent work. Thank you for visiting!
